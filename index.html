About MLSAC BVP

Empowering the next generation of tech leaders

We are Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors at Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, creating a vibrant community of innovators. Through hands-on workshops, cutting-edge hackathons, and collaborative projects, we foster technical excellence and leadership skills.

Our Core

Mission

Using Microsoft resources to help BVCOE advance its programming and development culture. To cherish progressive conversations upon holistic topics and providing networking opportunities with Microsoft MVPs or working professionals at Microsoft. Furthermore, improving teamwork and communal feelings, as well as the technical aspects of everyone working together.

Vision

Forming a network of thinkers who come together under the common dome of Microsoft's vision and technology to have open-minded discussions. Planning to cultivate the young talent at college to compete at global level competitions (like Microsoft Imagine Cup) thus heightening the name of BVCOE. To provide a free-learning experience to Microsoft's resources so that the technology is accessible to everyone at the college and beyond.

Our Signature Events

BugSmash

A two-round debugging showdown where participants first hunted down sneaky errors on paper and then optimized messy code. Only the sharpest minds conquered the bugs!

Tech, Rumble aur Panchayat

A high-energy tech battle featuring intense group discussions, rapid-fire domain-based questions, and a fierce cross-questioning round. The best tech minds went head-to-head!

CreatiCon

A fusion of ideation and design! Teams bid for their preferred domain, pitched groundbreaking ideas, and crafted unique logos. Innovation and creativity ruled the day!

DS' Rush

A fast-paced, offline DSA challenge where solo coders raced against time to solve complex problems. A true test of speed and logic!

Web-D-Verse

A web development face-off where teams first received expert mentoring, then showcased their creativity by designing a logo for their project. A playground for web wizards!

Tech Mon Tech

A thrilling tech treasure hunt! Participants cracked technical riddles from various domains, unlocking codes to progress through each stage. The fastest problem-solver claimed victory in this ultimate CTF-style challenge!
