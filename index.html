About MLSAC BVP

Empowering the next generation of tech leaders

We are Microsoft Learn Student Ambassadors at Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, creating a vibrant community of innovators. Through hands-on workshops, cutting-edge hackathons, and collaborative projects, we foster technical excellence and leadership skills.

Our Core

Mission

To empower students with cutting-edge technical skills through innovative learning platforms, fostering a culture of collaboration and technological excellence that bridges academia with industry needs.

Vision

To become the premier student-led technical community recognized for nurturing future-ready professionals who drive technological innovation and digital transformation on a global scale.
